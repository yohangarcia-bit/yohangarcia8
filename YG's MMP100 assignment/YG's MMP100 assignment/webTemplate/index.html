Akaya's Origin

By Yohan Garcia

Ayaka’s Origin is an interactive visual narrative that explores trauma, discipline, and purpose through motion, imagery, and atmosphere. The project is inspired by anime storytelling and cinematic framing, using dark tones, strong contrast, and deliberate pacing to reflect Ayaka’s emotional journey after the loss of her father.
